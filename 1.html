Explore Rajasthan

Jaipur - The Pink City

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is famous for its stunning palaces, vibrant bazaars, and historic forts. Must-visit attractions include the Hawa Mahal, Amer Fort, and Jantar Mantar.

Know more about Jaipur

Udaipur - The City of Lakes

Udaipur is known for its picturesque lakes and majestic palaces. The City Palace and Lake Pichola are top attractions here, offering a mix of history and natural beauty.

Know more about Udaipur

Jaisalmer - The Golden City

Located in the heart of the Thar Desert, Jaisalmer is famous for its golden sandstone architecture, including the Jaisalmer Fort and desert safaris.

Know more about Jaisalmer

Pushkar - The Holy Town

Pushkar is a spiritual town known for its sacred Pushkar Lake and the annual animal (mostly camel) fair. The Brahma Temple is another key highlight here.

Know more about Pushkar
